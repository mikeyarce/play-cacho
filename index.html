Play Cacho

Total: 0

Instructions:

  1. Click "Roll" to roll the 5 dice.
  2. Select the dice you want to keep by clicking on them.
  3. You can roll up to 3 times but you don't have to roll more than once.
  4. Dice you select will not be re-rolled.
  5. When you have your final dice selection, click "Add Score" which will highlight available scoring slots.
  6. Select your scoring slot. The totals are calculated automatically.
  7. You can select an area that will net you 0 points.
  8. Clicking "Clear" will reset your roll.
  9. Click "Start Over" at the bottom for a new game.
